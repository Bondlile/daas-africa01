ABOUT US

DAAS Africa provides agriculture and infrastructure data for the use of businesses and governments. We gather and collect data across Africa, to assist in evidence-based decision making. Our datasets allow businesses and governments to utilize data as a decision making tool, to achieve more impactful results.
